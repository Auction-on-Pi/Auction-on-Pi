Privacy Policy for Auction-on-Pi

Effective Date: April 22, 2025

1. Introduction

Auction-on-Pi is a mobile app that enables users to participate in auctions using Pi currency. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect:

  • Personal info (e.g., name, email, phone number) when you register or bid.
  • Payment details for auction transactions.
  • Device info (e.g., IP address, device type) for analytics.
  • Bidding history to improve our services.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use data to:

  • Process bids and payments.
  • Send auction notifications (e.g., bid confirmations, win notices).
  • Improve app functionality and user experience.

4. Data Sharing

We share data with:

  • Payment processors (e.g., Stripe) to complete transactions.
  • Analytics providers to understand app usage.
  • The Pi Network (if applicable) for currency transactions.

5. Your Rights

You can access, update, or delete your data by contacting us at zengenilewis@gmail.com. EU residents have additional rights under GDPR.

6. Security

We use encryption to protect your data, but no method is 100% secure.

7. Contact Us

For questions, email zengenilewis@gmail.com.

8. Changes

We may update this policy and will notify you via email or app notifications.
